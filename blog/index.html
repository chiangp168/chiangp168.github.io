Camping at Salmon La Sac Campground

As you may already know, I love going camping in the summer of Seattle. It has my favorite kind of weather. A weather with sunny blue sky, gental breeze and most importantly, 0% chance of rain. In the summer of 2020, we went camping at Salmon Lac Sac, a campground only 2 hours away from Bellevue.

As soon as we set up our tent at the campground, we went on a hike to Iron Bear - Teanaway Ridge Trail. I indeed, saw a bear there!

The trail is 6.5 miles round trip, and 1900ft gain in elevation. As always, all the sweat and hardwork became worth it when reaching the top of the mountain.

We also took a dip in the natural swimming hole. It took us an hour before we could finally submerge our whole body under this cold snow water from the top of the mountain.

Of course, the camping trip is not complete without a bonefire and smores.

I also highly recommend going to Cooper Lake for a tranquil lake experience. Perfect for kayaking or fishing!
